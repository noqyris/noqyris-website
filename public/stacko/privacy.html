STACKO — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

STACKO is a casual stacking game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

STACKO does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game progress (scores, coins, settings) is stored locally on your device using localStorage and is never sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

STACKO may display advertisements through third-party ad networks. These services may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

STACKO does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The game is suitable for all ages (rated 4+).

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at stackogame@gmail.com
